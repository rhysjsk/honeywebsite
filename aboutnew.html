Bees

Bees are important to our survival. Annually, the crops they pollinate are worth about 256 million (US) dollars. Without them, the food we take for granted would cease to exist.

However, recently, millions of hives have started to die out. A phenomenon known as "Colony Collapse Disorder" has affected bees all around the world. Although the main cause is not certain, there may be several factors that may cause this phenomenon, including insecticides, fungi, viruses and parasites.

All our profits will go to Motor Neurone Disease Victoria.

Every year in Australia, two people with Motor Neurone Disease die, and another two are diagnosed.

Many more are affected by losing their friends or family.
